A "good" shot is one that achieves the intended outcome before hitting the shot and a "bad" shot is everything else.

Example "good" shot definitions:

Drive = Stops in the Fairway at normal diving distance.

Longiron = A good strike which travels the expected distance in the intended direction.

Approach = Stops on the green.

Chip = Stops on the green within putting distance.

Putt = No more than 2 putts per hole and 1 putt if the first putt is less than 2 meters from the hole.
