Local Golf League - Guidelines & Format

Playing Matches:

League matches can be played at any golfcourse,
as long as all players in a leaguematch are playing the same course at the same time.

Use teeofftimes or similar to secure a good price for your round.

Once a league has started, a Whatsapp group will be set up to arrange matches.

(You can play up to 3 leaguematches at the same time when playing in a 4 ball).

Competition Format:

The first league matches will be played without handicaps - the player with the lowest total score wins.

2 points for a win, 1 point for a draw and 0 points for a loss.

All members of the league should play each other once within a set time period (e.g. 6 weeks for a league of 10 players).

The league will end when all matches have been played or the time limit has been exceeded.

Inputting Data:

Record a leaguematch by selecting "New Golf Round+" and inputting your golfscore as normal.
Then add your opponent's userid and their totalscore to record the match.

If you play the same person more than once, only the first match should be recorded as a league match.

Prerequisites:

Handicaps or membership of a golfclub are not required.

Golfers of all standards are encouraged to join the league.
