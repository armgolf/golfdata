Date of policy: 30/05/2017

Information Collection and Use

Registration

A username and password is requested to use the website.
The username and password are used to identify the user.

Golf League Signup

The user's phonenumber is requested when signing up to participate in a golf league.
The phonenumber is used to set up a Whatsapp group for the league which is used to schedule league matches.

Golf Activity Data

The following data can be entered by the user when recording golf data:
Course played, score per hole, a "good", "bad" or "N/a" rating for each shot type per hole, opponent played and the date the data is entered to the website on.
The golf activity data is used to operate the golf league and provide a golf data management service for the user.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Local Golf League does not share data in the "Information Collection and Use" section above with 3rd parties.
We reserve the right to disclose your personally identifiable information as required by law or when we believe that disclosure is necessary to protect person,
property, our rights and/or to comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process served on the Web Site and/or as permitted by applicable laws.
